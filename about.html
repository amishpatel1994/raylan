Indigo Employment Services Inc. is a market-focused staffing firm established in 2017. We have experienced staff with over 10 years of industry experience offering innovative staffing solutions to our clients. Like any successful business, the cornerstone behind our success is our workforce. We strive to find the right candidates with right set of skills that will help business exceed in their productivity and flexibility.

One of our pillars of success is recruiting with professionalism and passion. With an exclusive range of resource, we will offer our client’s a tailored-approach when it comes to match the right candidate for the job. We strive to become experts in your business. We do this by offering an honest and ethical approach to our clients. We use our extensive candidate database and various networking methods such as web-based networking, advertising and campaigns to allocate the right staff efficiency.

We understand that your time is valuable. Therefore, Indigo Employment Services Inc. will assign a dedicated in-house account manager to manage your specific staffing needs. Their sole focus will be to service your staffing requirements 24-7.

The benefits of having a dedicated recruiter are:

  • Assistance with on-boarding
  • Supporting supervisors
  • Assistance with new hire set-up
  • A direct point of contact for all temporary workers
  • Reduce employee turnover
